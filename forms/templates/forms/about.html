About

English

This website or application aims to help census workers to encode directly the data that will be inserted in this website, instead of having to write down the data in a paper and encode it later on.

Filipino

Itong website na ito ay ginawa upang tulungan ang mga census workers na i-encode na mismo sa website na ito ang anumang data na ilagay nila, imbis na kailanganing pang isulat sa papel at i-encode pagtapos.
